## Distilled product principles

These principles are distilled from the founder source above. They are not generic AI-productivity claims. They should inform product direction without flattening the founder's intent.

- OCC reduces unnecessary cognitive overhead around organizing, routing, monitoring, and deciding what deserves attention, so people can preserve energy for the work and life that matter most.
- OCC does not merely display work. It carries the state of the work with the user.

---

## Experience principles

- OCC should guide attention naturally while preserving user control, truthful tradeoffs, privacy boundaries, project-level overrides, and explicit route locks.
- The product should be humane, ambitious, calm, and built to serve users rather than consume their focus.

---

## Implementation-facing principles

Derived from the founder source for use by implementation chats, execution agents, and product contributors:

- The decision of where to focus belongs to OCC, not the user. The user chose what to build; OCC manages the how — so the user can stay on the why.
- Attention guidance must be natural, not interruptive. The system should lead rather than demand.
- OCC must not create additional cognitive work in the name of providing information. If a capability or interface adds overhead, it is moving in the wrong direction.
- Routing, monitoring, sequencing, and state-tracking are OCC's job. They must not surface as the user's burden.
- The quality bar is that a user with something meaningful to build should be able to focus on that meaning while OCC handles the operational mechanics.

## Candidate external-safe language

These messages are candidates only. They require deliberate founder review before public use. They must not appear in marketing copy, investor decks, public documentation, or external communications without that review.

- "OCC turns the state of your work into a space you can understand, navigate, and move through—so your energy stays on what matters most."
- "That is not a dashboard. That is visible orchestration."
- "A precision instrument built inside a sleeping nebula."

**Regarding the nebula:** The nebula reference is a behavior metaphor. It describes the quality of calm, contained, purposeful orchestration. It is not a literal space theme, a space-travel product narrative, or a visual requirement for astronomical imagery.

**Regarding provider language:** OCC works with and builds on a changing provider ecosystem. Candidate messaging must not disparage other AI providers. OCC is a stable layer above that ecosystem, not a competitor to any individual model or service.
